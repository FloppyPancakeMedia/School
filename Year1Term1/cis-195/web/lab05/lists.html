Albums From Waterfowl Dance

AHAOPA
A somewhat shocking, noise-filled affair created entirely with samples and free virtual instruments.
Everything In The Past Is...
A more refined, brooding affair than the previous. With a larger focus on lush orchestration, this focuses more on beauty and melancholy.
Blood Art
An entirely instrumental record, Blood Art aims to create evolving mood-pieces with a fictional band of drums, 2 guitars, bass, clarinet, flute, and viola.
An Elaborate Choreography of Failure
An extremely personal, and embarassing, vomiting about a life changing incident. This features more aggression than previous works, venturing more into post-hardcore territory than I might care to admit.
