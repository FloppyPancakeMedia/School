Here's the main content

Floppy Pancake Media is the production company headed by Cole McLain. It means nothing and we don't really do anything for the world, but it's a fun little platform under which to group various creative endeavors. From music releases, to coding projects, to board games we hope to more than half-finish, and whatever else, it is plopped under this umbrella, hopefully to be protected by the rain of a stampeding parade of critics, haters, and other bastards who just don't know what good stuff is. Because we, of course, don't, could not, and never will, make a bad product. Nope.

Conceived in 2020 or so, Floppy Pancake has really only released a handful of musical records from its only artist Waterfowl Dance. Waterfowl Dance is doomed to fade into obscurity, sitting solemnly on some server rack somewhere in who-the-fuck-knows until global disaster or the company goes bankrupt and wipes its drives clean for decommissioning and probably selling to poor bastards like myself. Still, we are a proud people... Or I'm a proud person... Who is this 'we' anyway? Anybody else notice the Almighty 'We' floating in the abyss?

Anyway, we also did a game once. It was called Market of Mayhem 2. There was no Market of Mayhem 1. Why make 1 when you could skip straight to 2? Unfortunately, the vision never saw full fruition although the project may be resurrected for sake of portfolio. It was a 3D arena brawler, just a chaotic mess of grocery store employees punching and throwing food at each other. But mostly we do music. Thanks for visiting.
